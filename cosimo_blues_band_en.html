Cosimo Blues Band

As a quartet, with Olivier Ingargiola on drums, Claude Giovanelle on bass, and Christian Piton on harmonica.

Philippe Thevenin, Eric Berger, Lolo Beauchier, Marcel Roche, and Romuald Lopinto have played and still do sporadically play the sticks.

Lionel Mollo on the harmonica was involved for 12 years. Elli Coquard also played a few notes with us.

Luc Mas still plays bass occasionally, and Sylvain Laurent was there for the first five years.
